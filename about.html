Before You Dive In

The Unfiltered Story

I am Zain, and my career has never followed a straight line. It looks more like a map drawn by someone who got lost but kept walking confidently. I began young in the automotive world and grew into a Lead Technician, spending years fixing problems, diagnosing faults, mentoring apprentices, and wondering why cars always break at the worst possible moment.

Life took me through different jobs. Food service taught me patience. Royal Mail taught me speed. Security taught me awareness. Debt recovery taught me emotional strength and how to stay calm in conversations nobody enjoys. Every role shaped me.

After many years as a Lead Technician, I wanted something new. I always enjoyed technology, so I moved into IT. Now I help people with computers, software, and printers that behave like they have personal issues.

At the same time, I started studying Artificial Intelligence. It is confusing and difficult, but rewarding. I am learning Python, machine learning, and new ways to think. Some days the code listens, other days it argues, but that is part of the journey.

I do not pretend to have life figured out. I focus on learning, improving, and staying grounded. Outside work, I enjoy food, quiet walks, travelling, horse riding, and moments of peace.

If you reached this far, thank you. My story is simple, honest, and still in progress.
