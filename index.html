A Few Things About Me

I started my working life in the automotive world, slowly growing into a Lead Technician. I spent years diagnosing strange noises, fixing complicated issues, and watching apprentices discover new ways to drop tools.

I worked in security, food service, Royal Mail, and debt recovery. Every role taught me something new about people, patience, and how to stay calm when life feels chaotic.

Now I work as an IT Technician, helping people with their technology, and repeating the classic words "Have you tried turning it off and on again" far more than I imagined.

I am also studying Artificial Intelligence, which means I willingly signed up to argue with Python at two in the morning. Progress is progress.
